Our Current Programs

Homeschool Co-op

Join our Homeschool Co-op program where students immerse themselves in a comprehensive education that integrates the Core Knowledge Curriculum with language enhancements in Arabic and Latin. Our dedicated instructors provide personalized attention and engaging lessons to ensure students thrive in their learning journey.


Classes are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. In addition to academic subjects, students will have the opportunity to participate in enriching activities and build a strong sense of community.

After-School Quran Learning

Enhance your child's understanding of the Quran with our after-school Quran learning sessions. These sessions are designed to provide students with a deeper connection to their faith and a profound understanding of the teachings within the Quran.


We offer two convenient time slots for the after-school Quran learning sessions:


  • Tuesdays and Thursdays from 3:30 PM to 4:30 PM
  • Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM

During these sessions, students will engage in meaningful discussions, reflections, and study of the Quranic verses, fostering a strong sense of spirituality and knowledge.
