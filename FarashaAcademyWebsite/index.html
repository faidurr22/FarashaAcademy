Our Academy


At Farasha Academy, we are dedicated to fostering a rich educational experience that blends tradition with innovation. Our academy focuses on teaching Arabic through the acclaimed book "Arabic Between your Hands" and the profound teachings of the Qur'an.


In addition to our language offerings, we proudly embrace the Core Knowledge Curriculum, providing students with a well-rounded education that spans various subjects while nurturing their Arabic and Latin language skills.

Our Offerings


Co-op Style Homeschooling


Join our co-op style homeschool program, where students embark on an enriching journey through the Core Knowledge Curriculum. Our unique approach integrates Arabic and Latin as language enhancements, creating a holistic educational experience.

After School Program


Our after school program specializes in teaching "Arabic Between your Hands" and delving into the profound wisdom of the Qur'an. Enroll your child in this program to provide them with a strong foundation in Arabic language and Islamic studies.
